My Time as a Treasurer

Nepalese Student Association

Summer of 23', I was elected as the Treasurer for HUNSA. It was amazing.

Orientation @ Mountain View

From the moment I checked into Bay View Suites, it was non-stop action. The orientation itinerary was packed, but they also had optional activities peppered in. I jumped on the chance for a gBike ride (Google's own bikes) and a guided tour of the campus – it's like a mini-city!

Over the next few days, we dove into keynote speeches, icebreakers, and deep dives into topics like the Google hiring process and the detailed world of product management. We had a chance to visit the legendary Googleplex, which was just as awe-inspiring as you'd expect. Our schedule even included a few fitness breaks, which was a huge help after all those hours of travel.

Orientation week wasn't all work and no play. Our final day included a trip to San Francisco, complete with a group photo under the iconic Golden Gate Bridge. Exploring the city and a scavenger hunt gave us a taste of the Bay Area's energy. The cherry on top was the Warriors game – what an experience!
