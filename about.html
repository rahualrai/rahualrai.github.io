About

Hi! I'm Rahual Rai, a Computer Science student originally from Singapore and currently based in the Washington, D.C. Join me on my journey, accompanied by my coffee mug and trusty laptop, as I explore the world of technology and beyond.

View My Resume

I grew up under the glittering skyline of Singapore, a city where technology and tradition coexist in harmony. As a child, I was captivated by the seamless integration of advanced technology in everyday life. It wasn't just the gadgets and the gizmos that intrigued me, but the underlying code that breathed life into them. This early exposure to the digital world sparked a flame in me, a burning curiosity to unravel the mysteries of computer science.

My journey took an unexpected turn when I moved to Nepal for high school. The serene landscapes and rich cultural tapestry of Nepal offered a stark contrast to Singapore's urban bustle. It was here, amid the towering Himalayas, that I learned to appreciate the beauty of diversity and the value of different perspectives. The lessons I learned in Nepal went beyond textbooks; they were about resilience, adaptability, and the art of finding balance.

At Howard University, my dive into computer science and math went beyond academics. It was about nurturing ideas and shaping the future. My diverse projects and research taught me to shape technology responsibly. Standing at this juncture, my journey from Singapore's vibrancy, through Nepal's serenity, to Howard's innovation, has been enriching. Looking forward, I embrace the mantra: "Every end is a new adventure's beginning."
